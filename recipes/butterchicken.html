Butter Chicken

Description

Butter Chicken, or Murgh Makhani, is a beloved Indian dish featuring succulent marinated chicken in a velvety tomato-based curry enriched with butter and aromatic spices. This standalone delight doesn't need naan or rice, offering a rich and satisfying culinary experience with every bite.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Tomatoes
  • Butter
  • Heavy cream
  • Aromatic spices: Garam masala, Cumin, Coriander
  • Fresh cilantro and scallions (for garnishing)

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken in heavy cream and a spice mixture of your choice.
  2. Cook the marinated chicken until tender.
  3. Put your tomatos trough a food processor.
  4. Add butter, heavy cream, and your aromatic spices to the mixture.
  5. Combine the cooked chicken with the mixture and cook in a pan until thickened.
  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro.
